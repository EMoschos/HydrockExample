About HYDROCK

Aspiring to be leaders in all facets of drilling operations whether it be in mining, exploration, or oil and gas. We pride ourselves on groundbreaking drilling solutions that provide the best possible products, service, and value for money to our clients Australia wide. WHAT WE OFFER

HYDROCK have a no limits philosophy towards all aspects of mineral drilling. If you are looking for specialists in reverse circulation (RC), air core, water bore construction, or diamond drilling, we will provide drilling professionals, and equipment suited to each client's needs, no matter the size, complexity, or location of the drilling program.
